# pccompiler

OVERVIEW:

A PC part picker prograrm, compile your new PC step by step, component after component.

The system lets your rerview items by Category (CPU, Cooler, Motheboard etc...).
For each category you'll get a various components from different manufacturers of all kinds.
You can add, remove, replace items from your cart.
You may build a full pc by choosing all the components required,
    or just the components you need, if you want to upgrade some part.
    


HOW TO USE:

- Extract content of zip file.
- Launch Android Studio, update gradle if required (sdk, etc).
- May run on any device or any simulator.
- Signup, freely and might take a few seconds, no more.
- To create new compilation of components, click 'compile' button.
- Explore the application interface.



NOTE:

It's just the prototype version, nothing official.
